Spent a long weekend in Vienna where I spent time enjoying the tasty treats in the [Stadtpark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stadtpark,_Vienna) at the [Genuss Festival](https://www.genussregionen.at). Did you know Austria has a [Wine Quarter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weinviertel)?! The rest of the time was spent exploring the city, visiting its many museums around the [Museumsplatz ](https://www.wien.info/en/museumsquartier-overview-architecture-tour-431168)including the [Pieter Bruegel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pieter_Bruegel_the_Elder) exhibition at the [Kunsthistorisches Museum](https://www.wien.info/en/art-culture/museums-exhibitions/top-museums/kunsthistorisches-museum-350470#). Many a chuckle was had at early interpretations of animals artists had clearly not seen – but who am I to judge? The crux of the trip was around a performance of [Mahler's](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gustav_Mahler) [8th Symphony](https://open.spotify.com/album/2PCu6ocEbTwFmjcBN8kuwO?si=SbXN3_FrSmG8T4MWgQC27Q) at the [Konzerthaust](https://konzerthaus.at/de). Vienna was hit by huge thunderstorms that afternoon, and it was possible to hear the thunder inside the venue. 

Favourite restaurant - hooray for having outdoor space on a Sunday evening! - was [MAST](https://www.mast.wine): we enjoyed the six course Chef's Choice ([PDF menu](https://www.mast.wine/files/Dinner-MAST.pdf)) and accompanying wine flight…
