Another week down. What's new?

I've been watching the two of them bring little bits of fluff to make it their own. How long will they spend making the nest? When do they lay their eggs? How long do they brood? When's Springwatch back on? I feel like I've hit middle-age in the past few months: I've discovered my green fingers; I enjoy watching birds; and I'm started developing a taste for wine.
